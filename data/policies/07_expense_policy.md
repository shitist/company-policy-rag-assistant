# Expense Policy

Sample policy document for a fictional organization. This file contains no real company data.

## Purpose

This policy describes sample rules for work-related expense reimbursement. It uses abstract tiers and avoids real financial thresholds.

## Eligible Expenses

Eligible expenses include pre-planned work travel, approved production supplies, software used for project work, and event-related materials that are tied to a documented business purpose.

## Expense Tiers

Expenses are grouped into abstract tiers:

- Tier A: routine low-risk expenses.
- Tier B: moderate expenses requiring additional explanation.
- Tier C: unusual, urgent, or high-risk expenses requiring documented review.

The demo does not define real currency amounts. Any implementation must avoid realistic company thresholds.

## Receipts and Notes

Each reimbursement request should include a receipt or equivalent record, date, category, business purpose, and requester note. Missing receipts should be flagged for review rather than automatically approved.

## Non-eligible Expenses

Personal items, unclear entertainment expenses, duplicate claims, unsupported subscriptions, and items unrelated to the work purpose should not be reimbursed.
