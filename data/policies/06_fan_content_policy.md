# Fan Content Usage Policy

Sample policy document for a fictional organization. This file contains no real company data.

## Purpose

This policy defines sample rules for handling fan-submitted comments, artwork, edits, photos, videos, and messages in a media environment.

## Viewing vs Reusing

Staff may view public fan content for community awareness, but viewing content does not create permission to reuse it. Reposting, editing, quoting, including in promotional material, or using as campaign inspiration should be treated as a separate use case.

## Permission Record

Before reuse, staff should keep a permission record that includes the creator handle or placeholder, the requested use, the response, the allowed platform, and any attribution requirement. If the permission record is missing, unclear, or limited to a different use, the content should not be reused.

## Sensitive Fan Submissions

Fan content that includes minors, private locations, medical topics, harassment, personal disputes, or private talent information should not be amplified by official channels. Staff should preserve context if needed for review, but should avoid public engagement that increases visibility.

## Examples

A positive comment may be liked from an official account if it does not contain private information. A fan artwork image should not be included in a campaign banner unless the permission record allows that specific use.
