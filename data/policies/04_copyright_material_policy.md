# Copyright Material Usage Policy

Sample policy document for a fictional organization. This file contains no real company data.

## Purpose

This policy describes sample rules for using images, audio, video, text, and design assets in a content organization.

## Approved Source Types

Material may be used when it comes from an approved asset library, an original work, a clearly documented license, or a partner package that explicitly includes the intended use. The allowed use must match the platform, territory, duration, and format described in the record.

## Restricted Source Types

Staff must not assume that material found through search engines, social posts, fan accounts, screenshots, or informal messages is available for reuse. Watermarks, credits, or public visibility do not create permission in this demo policy.

## Attribution and Records

When material is reused, the content owner should keep a record of source, allowed use, modification status, and attribution requirement. If the record is missing or unclear, the material should not be used in public-facing content.

## AI-Generated Material

AI-generated images, music, scripts, or other creative assets should be reviewed before use. Review should consider prompt inputs, similarity risk, rights claims, platform rules, and whether the output contains recognizable protected elements.
